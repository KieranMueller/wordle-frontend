this is a game of wordle

  • you are trying to guess a pre-defined word
  • the length of the word equals the amount of boxes in a row
  • each row is one guess, if there are 6 rows, you have 6 attempts
  • the goal is to guess the word in as few attempts as possible
  • tiles that turn green represent letters in the same position as the secret word
  • tiles that turn orange are present in the secret word, but not in that position
  • tiles that remain the color of the board, and turn gray on the keyboard, are not in the secret word
  • click here to create a secret word for a friend to guess!
